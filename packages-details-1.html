There is no better way to experience and enjoy the renowned California Wine Country than in a small group with an experienced and knowledgeable guide/driver! There is a magical fascination to the year-long wine-making process; from the quiet winters to the ripe excitement of the fall harvest. Discover it for your-self...with us.

No big groups, no worries - a feast for the senses! Beautiful scenery, friendly people, some of the world's best wines and a delicious lunch! It doesn't get any better than this. Tasting fees and restaurant lunch are included in our wine tour.

Imagine yourself in the beautiful Wine Country in a small group in one of our customized mini vans, leisurely going from charming winery to charming winery ...

Your knowledgeable guide/driver taking you to the best places:

  • explaining the technique and art of wine making ...
  • seeing both the Napa and Sonoma valleys ...
  • stopping for lunch at a cozy restaurant with staff waiting to serve you ...
  • and ending the day with a bit of the bubbly at a table reserved for you.

Your only job is to sit back and enjoy a splendid day! This is what awaits you on a RIZWANA SAFARIS USATour of the Wine Country.
